Kensho

As the lead UX engineer at Kensho, I was tasked with making the visualization and quantitative analysis of thousands of financial data points a smooth and friendly user experience. As a result, users are able to draw meaningful conclusions in a matter of minutes, something that would take days to do with traditional software. I managed my own projects from pencil wireframes to pixel perfect mockups to lighting fast front end JavaScript, HTML5, and CSS3. During my tenure the company grew from 3 to 30 people, received funding from Google and Goldman Sachs, and increased in value from $500k to $150M.
